FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is OTC trading?

OTC trading is a private, off-exchange trade between two parties, typically for large volumes.

How do I get started?

Contact us via WhatsApp or Telegram to begin the KYC process.

What currencies do you support?

We support USD, EUR, UGX, KES, and major cryptocurrencies.

Is my money safe?

Yes, we employ institutional-grade security, encryption, and segregated accounts.

What are the fees?

Fees are transparent and quoted based on volume and market conditions.
